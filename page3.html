OneOnOne

collaboration with Mahdieh Fahimi

Dec 2014, video/ performance 4’ 13”

During this video there were two performances, one behind the camera performed by Mahdieh and one in front of the camera which I performed. I searched for a composition in my actions, while Mahdieh was searching for the composition with the camera. We visited the location at different times and made several videos. After each filming session we tried to come to conclusions about what we were trying to achieve. We both liked that she did not know what my next action would be and I did not know what she was filming. After some time visiting the same location, there was a kind of energy from knowing the place but not knowing from one another what the next step would be. We wanted the same energy to be present in the video. At the end of the project we recorded our thoughts and added the footage to the final work.






Chapter Three

collaboration with Mahdieh Fahimi

Nov 2014, video / performance 3’ 47”

Artist Mahdieh Fahimi lives and works in Belgium. Her work consists of performance and video. Before the video we made together she asked me to perform in a video piece she was making: Chapter Three. During ‘Chapter Three’ Mahdieh chose a frame with her camera and used it as the starting point for different actions I did. I was expected to see this frame as a platform. Together we had discussions questioning what the definition of performance is and how video is used within a performance based art practice. Through this work we explored how video could be used not as just documentation of a performance, but as a final artwork in its own right.
