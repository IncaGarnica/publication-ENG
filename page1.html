Palinghuis

2015, video/ performance 

For this video I did an art residence in a building for one week as part of my practice.

This building consists of a restaurant and an appartment above it.

I see this work as an excersise of documenting different places in the building. I try to search a relation between the images I create with the camera and the way I put myself in these images.


Locker Open, Locker Closed 2015, 

2015, video/ performance/ installation 

In this work I search my way to archive all the objects in my room. I document the actions I take as a performer.

In every video at different times I open the locker, put an object in, closed it, opened it again, put another object in and closed it. This action goes on in each video. 



Holding Chair as Long as Possible in the Air Next to Tree

Nov 2014, Video/ Perfmormance 6’24”

For this work I went to the Netherlands. I wanted this action in a frame where you could see only a tree, a chair and me.

(I hold the projector in front of a wall of a large building in Ghent, Belgium. I was looking for a relation between the body, the video and the wall. --> ZIE TEKENING DIE NOG BIJGEVOEGD MOET WORDEN)
