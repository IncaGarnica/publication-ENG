Counting Out Loud Until The Camera Shuts Down

Apr 2014, video/ performance 12’41”

To celebrate LUCA Mixed Media studios eleventh anniversary our teachers and old students organised an exhibition in the contemporary art centre Netwerk Aalst. I created this video for the festival.

My fellow students and teachers also particapted in the festival each showing seven minute piece on a rectangular platform in the market square in Aalst, Belgium. As I was on a research trip and could not attend the festival in person this video was filmed in Ardennen, Wallonië, Belgium. Instead of the platform I used a chair and instead of the market I was surrounded by cows however we all performed at the same time.

Cardboard

Feb 2014, video/ performance/ installation

Actions for the performer/artist

1. Collecting Cardboard:

One day I wanted to make a big wall in the atelier of our studio (Mixed Media). The most efficient, cheapest and easiest way was using cardboard boxes. So I started to collect boxes.

2. Making a Wall With Cardboard:

After this experience of creating a wall I came to the conclusion that I was more interested in the process of creating a wall than the final result of my labour.

3. Four Hour Performance With Cardboard in Exibition Space:

During an exhibition I had the chance to put the process of making the wall into a performance. I started carrying every box I used for the wall through the building, from downstairs to the top floor. All of the boxes were initially thrown in one heap.

 4. Removing Cardboard 

For the final stage of this project I created an entire space of cardboard, using the boxes. When standing or sitting inside, you felt like you were in a huge cardboard box. To conclude the performance I distroyed the space. The energy dissapeared from the boxes and they became useless waste objects.
